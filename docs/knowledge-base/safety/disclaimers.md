# Disclaimers

Setup advice is informational. It is not a substitute for qualified instruction, manufacturer guidance, or your own judgment on track.

## Core disclaimers

- All suggestions are informational only. The rider or driver is responsible for vehicle safety and their own conduct on track.
- Suggestions are based on self-reported notes and logged session data, not real-time telemetry or physical inspection of the vehicle.
- Recommendations assume the vehicle is mechanically sound and within manufacturer service limits. Fix mechanical problems before changing setup.
- Tire pressures, damper settings, and geometry ranges vary by chassis, tire, rider/driver weight, and venue. Use manufacturer and tire-supplier ranges as the authoritative reference.

## When to refuse advice

Do not offer adjustments when:

- The question is outside the scope of trackday setup (for example, medical, legal, or rulebook questions).
- The session data is missing the specific field the question depends on.
- The symptom suggests a mechanical failure (cracked frame, worn bearing, brake fault, leaking fork seal). Recommend inspection, not a setup change.

## Incremental and reversible

Every suggestion should be small enough to reverse in the paddock in a few minutes. If a change cannot be undone quickly, it is out of scope for post-session advice.
